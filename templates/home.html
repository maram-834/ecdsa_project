Elliptic Curve Types

P256

P-256 is the most widely used elliptic curve. It offers strong security while remaining very fast, making it ideal for websites, mobile apps, and general-purpose cryptography.

P384

P-384 provides higher security than P-256. It is used when extra protection is required, such as government systems or long-term data security, while still maintaining good performance

P521

P-521 offers the highest security level among the standard curves. It is chosen for highly sensitive environments where maximum cryptographic strength is more important than speed.
